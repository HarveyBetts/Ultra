Your paces

Worked out from your 10km time, and kept up to date from Strava. For an ultra almost everything is run easy — easy pace is a skill, not a cop-out. Each run in your plan shows the pace to aim for.

Ask your coach

Your coach knows your plan, paces, goal and your why — and it can change your plan. Tell it "I do a 5km parkrun every Saturday" or "I can't run Thursdays" and it rewrites your weeks. Keep chatting as long as you like.
